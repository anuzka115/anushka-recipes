Taco Recipe

Description

These mouthwatering tacos are a delightful treat for taco enthusiasts. They are easy to make and can be filled with your favorite ingredients for a personalized touch.

Ingredients

  • 8 small taco shells or tortillas
  • 500g ground beef or your choice of protein (chicken, turkey, or tofu for vegetarians)
  • 1 packet of taco seasoning mix
  • 1 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup diced onions
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped cilantro
  • 1/4 cup sour cream
  • 1/4 cup salsa
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

Steps

  1. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat.
  2. Add the ground beef (or protein of your choice) and cook until it's browned and fully cooked.
  3. Drain excess fat if necessary, then add the taco seasoning mix to the cooked meat and stir well to combine. Let it simmer for a few minutes.
  4. Heat the taco shells or tortillas in the oven or microwave according to the package instructions.
  5. Fill each taco shell with the seasoned meat.
  6. Top the tacos with shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, diced onions, shredded cheddar cheese, and chopped cilantro.
  7. Add a dollop of sour cream and salsa on top of each taco.
  8. Serve the delicious tacos immediately and enjoy!
