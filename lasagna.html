Lasagna Recipe

Description

This classic lasagna recipe is a comforting and hearty dish that everyone will love. Layers of rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and melted cheese make this lasagna a family favorite.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 500g ground beef
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 800g canned crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 2 teaspoons dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 4 cups béchamel sauce (white sauce)

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large skillet, cook the ground beef over medium heat until browned. Drain excess fat if needed.
  3. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic to the skillet with the beef and sauté until the onions are soft and translucent.
  4. Stir in the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, dried oregano, dried basil, salt, and black pepper. Let the sauce simmer for about 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  5. In a large pot, boil the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions until they are al dente. Drain the noodles and set them aside.
  6. Spread a thin layer of the meat sauce on the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish.
  7. Place a layer of cooked lasagna noodles on top of the sauce.
  8. Add a layer of béchamel sauce on top of the noodles, spreading it evenly.
  9. Sprinkle some shredded mozzarella and grated Parmesan cheese over the béchamel sauce.
  10. Repeat steps 7-9 to create more layers (typically 3-4 layers) until you run out of ingredients, finishing with a layer of meat sauce and cheese on top.
  11. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes.
  12. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 15 minutes or until the cheese is bubbly and golden on top.
  13. Let the lasagna cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.
  14. Enjoy your delicious homemade lasagna!
