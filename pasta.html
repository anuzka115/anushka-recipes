Pasta Recipe

Description

This delicious pasta recipe is quick and easy to make. It's a perfect dish for any pasta lover and can be customized with your favorite ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 250g pasta (any type you prefer)
  • 2 cups of your favorite pasta sauce
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup chopped onions
  • 1/2 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 1/2 cup chopped tomatoes
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish

Steps

  1. Fill a large pot with water and add a pinch of salt. Bring it to a boil over high heat.
  2. Add the pasta to the boiling water and cook according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain the pasta and set it aside.
  3. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and chopped onions. Saut√© them until the onions become translucent.
  4. Add the sliced mushrooms and chopped tomatoes to the skillet. Cook until the mushrooms are tender.
  5. Pour the pasta sauce into the skillet and stir well. Let it simmer for a few minutes.
  6. Add the cooked pasta to the sauce and toss until it's well coated.
  7. Sprinkle grated Parmesan cheese over the pasta and mix it in.
  8. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  9. Garnish with fresh basil leaves.
  10. Serve hot and enjoy your delicious pasta!
